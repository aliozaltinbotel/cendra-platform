# Standard Operating Procedure: Property Cleaning

## Overview
This SOP applies to all turnover cleanings between guest stays. Follow this checklist in order to ensure consistency and quality.

## Time Allocation
- **2-bedroom apartment**: 90 minutes
- **1-bedroom apartment**: 60 minutes
- **Studio**: 45 minutes
- Add 15 minutes for each additional bathroom

## Cleaning Order

### 1. Initial Walkthrough (5 minutes)
- Take BEFORE photos of all rooms (document current state)
- Check for obvious damage or missing items
- Note any guest belongings left behind (place in Lost & Found bag)
- Open all windows for ventilation

### 2. Bed Linens & Bedrooms (20 minutes per bedroom)
- Strip all beds completely (sheets, pillowcases, duvet covers)
- Check mattress protector for stains — replace if soiled
- Inspect pillows and duvets — replace if stained or damaged
- Make beds with fresh linens (hospital corners required)
- Dust all surfaces: nightstands, headboard, dresser, lamps
- Wipe mirrors and glass surfaces
- Empty and wipe trash cans
- Vacuum floors and under beds
- Check closets: ensure hangers are evenly spaced, shelf is clean

### 3. Bathrooms (15 minutes per bathroom)
- Remove all used towels and bath mats
- Scrub toilet (inside bowl, seat, base, behind)
- Clean shower/tub: tiles, grout, glass door, fixtures
- Clean sink, faucet, and vanity countertop
- Clean mirror (streak-free)
- Mop floor
- Restock: toilet paper (2 rolls + 1 spare), hand soap, shampoo, conditioner, body wash
- Hang fresh towels: 2 bath towels, 2 hand towels, 1 bath mat per guest
- Check drain for hair — clean if needed

### 4. Kitchen (20 minutes)
- Wash all dishes or run dishwasher
- Clean and sanitize all countertops
- Clean stovetop, oven exterior, and range hood
- Clean microwave (inside and outside)
- Wipe down refrigerator (inside shelves, remove expired items)
- Clean sink and faucet
- Take out trash and replace bag
- Wipe cabinet fronts
- Restock: dish soap, sponge, paper towels, trash bags
- Check coffee maker — clean and add fresh supplies (coffee, filters, sugar)

### 5. Living Areas (15 minutes)
- Dust all surfaces: TV, shelves, coffee table, side tables
- Fluff and arrange couch cushions and throw pillows
- Fold and drape throw blankets neatly
- Wipe TV screen and remote controls (sanitize remote)
- Vacuum all floors and rugs
- Check under couch cushions for debris

### 6. Floors Throughout (10 minutes)
- Vacuum all carpeted areas
- Mop all hard floors (wood, tile, laminate)
- Spot-clean any visible stains on carpet
- Check baseboards and corners for dust

### 7. Final Check (5 minutes)
- Set thermostat to 72°F (22°C)
- Turn off all lights except entry light
- Ensure all doors and windows are locked
- Check WiFi is working (connect and test)
- Reset smart lock access code for next guest
- Take AFTER photos of all rooms
- Complete the cleaning checklist in the app
- Lock the door and confirm via smart lock

## Quality Standards
- **No hair**: Check all surfaces, floors, and drains
- **No streaks**: Mirrors, glass, and stainless steel must be streak-free
- **No odors**: Property should smell fresh (use approved air freshener only)
- **Fully stocked**: All consumables at specified levels
- **Photo documentation**: Minimum 8 photos (each room + kitchen + bathroom)

## Issue Reporting
If you find any of the following, report IMMEDIATELY before cleaning:
- Property damage (furniture, walls, fixtures, appliances)
- Stains that cannot be removed with standard cleaning
- Missing items (electronics, kitchen appliances, decorations)
- Pest evidence (droppings, bugs, nests)
- Safety hazards (broken glass, exposed wires, water leaks)
