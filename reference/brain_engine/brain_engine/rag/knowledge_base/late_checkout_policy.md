# Late Checkout Policy

## Standard Checkout Time
- **Standard checkout**: 11:00 AM
- **Standard check-in**: 3:00 PM (next guest)
- **Turnover window**: 4 hours (11 AM - 3 PM)

## Late Checkout Fee Schedule

| Extension     | Fee   | Latest Checkout | Notes                          |
|---------------|-------|-----------------|--------------------------------|
| 1-2 hours     | $50   | 1:00 PM         | Subject to availability        |
| 2-4 hours     | $100  | 3:00 PM         | Requires cleaner schedule adjustment |
| 4+ hours      | N/A   | N/A             | Not available — conflicts with next guest |

## Approval Criteria

### Automatically Approved (if conditions met)
- Extension of 1-2 hours AND no same-day check-in
- Guest has 4.5+ star rating on Airbnb
- Guest requests at least 12 hours in advance

### Requires Manual Review
- Extension of 2-4 hours
- Same-day turnover with next guest
- Guest has below 4.5 star rating
- Request made less than 12 hours before checkout

### Automatically Denied
- Requested checkout time is later than 4:00 PM
- Requested checkout time is within 2 hours of next guest check-in
- Guest has previous late checkout violations
- Property has back-to-back bookings with no buffer

## Process

### Step 1: Receive Request
- Log the request with timestamp
- Note requested checkout time
- Check next guest's check-in time

### Step 2: Check Feasibility
- Calculate available buffer time
- Verify cleaner availability for adjusted window
- Check if fee schedule applies

### Step 3: Negotiate (if applicable)
- Inform guest of the late checkout fee
- Confirm they agree to the charge
- Document agreement

### Step 4: Confirm & Coordinate
- Update the smart lock schedule (extend access code validity)
- Notify the cleaning crew of adjusted arrival time
- Notify the next guest if check-in buffer is reduced
- Add the late checkout fee to the reservation extras

## Cleaner Impact
- Late checkout reduces the cleaning window
- If checkout is extended to 1:00 PM, cleaner arrives at 1:00 PM (2-hour window)
- If checkout is extended to 3:00 PM, cleaner arrives at 3:00 PM (compressed cleaning)
- Minimum cleaning time: 90 minutes for 2BR property
- If cleaning window drops below 90 minutes, consider delaying next guest check-in

## Communication Templates

### Approval Message
> "Hi [Guest Name], we'd be happy to extend your checkout to [Time]. There is a $[Fee] late checkout fee that will be added to your reservation. The new checkout time has been confirmed. Please enjoy the extra time!"

### Denial Message
> "Hi [Guest Name], unfortunately we're unable to accommodate a late checkout to [Time] as our next guest is arriving shortly after. Our standard checkout time is 11:00 AM. If you need a bit of extra time, we may be able to offer a [shorter extension] for $[Fee]. Let me know if that would help!"

### Fee Negotiation
> "The late checkout fee for a [X]-hour extension is $[Fee]. This covers the additional cleaning coordination needed. Would you like to proceed with the late checkout?"
